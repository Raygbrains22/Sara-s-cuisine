Jollof Trivia

When did jollof become a dish for many countries?

The dish's origins are traced to the Senegambian region. Regional variations are a source of competition between the countries of West Africa, and in particular Nigeria and Ghana, over whose version is the best; in the 2010s this developed into a friendly rivalry known as the "Jollof Wars". Hence Jollof rice became part of the main dish of many countries since 2010. (Source: www.google.com)
